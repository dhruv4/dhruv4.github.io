Hey! I am a senior at Thomas Jefferson High School for Science and Technology. I am passionate about computer science, videography, music, and public speaking. I love to research and have several prize-winning computer science, economics and biology science fair projects, with a few patents pending.

Currently I'm an intern in the DASLab at Harvard University under Dr. Stratos Idreos. I am also the Founder and CEO of Dhoom Dance, a Bollywood Dance School, and Guptapps, an application development firm.

I'm the President of TJHSST Namaste, the largest high school cultural club in America, which hosts iNite - a day-long celebration of culture. I've also built several web applications such as Vend Now and DJ Share. With a partner, I developed an algorithm that predicts stock price movements with an 80% accuracy by analyzing social media data. Finally, I'm an avid Public Forum debater with several national, state, and local awards.
