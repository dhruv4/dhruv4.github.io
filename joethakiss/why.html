From Main Street to Wall Street

Joe started off just like you. A blue collar worker while he was in high school in Nantucket, MA. His family was a typical middle-class American household, where his father ran a small business and his mother was a professor at Wellesley.

But, Joe was ambitious. And, rising from his humble beginnings, Joe became so much more. A truly productive, value-generating American. Armed with his Harvard degree and Yale MBA, and a small half a million loan from his father, Joe rolled up his sleeves and got to work.
